Overview

BSE Electronic manufactures aviation obstruction lighting systems used across Thailand. The challenge was not only product quality, but how to explain complex technical systems clearly on a website.

Rekha Ganita is an artisan reed diffuser brand built entirely from scratch. The concept draws from 8 celestial archetypes — Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, and a hidden eighth — each translated into a unique scent profile and visual identity. The brand targets a niche introvert audience that values intentionality, quiet luxury, and meaningful objects. Everything — from the naming system to the packaging concept to the Scent Oracle quiz — was designed to create a world, not just sell a product. This is a personal project that demonstrates the full range of brand-building skills: strategy, identity, digital, and product.

My Role

I built Rekha Ganita entirely solo — from brand naming and archetype strategy, to visual identity, product design, scent profile development, e-commerce build, and content system. Every touchpoint was designed and executed by one person.

The Challenge

The artisan home fragrance market is crowded with generic "luxury" aesthetics — marble textures, gold fonts, and vague wellness claims. Standing out requires a genuinely different world.

The challenge was building a brand with enough depth and personality that customers feel like they're discovering something, not just buying a product. And doing it with zero team — strategy, design, copy, development, and product all handled solo.

The Outcome

The brand launched with a live e-commerce store, a functioning Scent Oracle quiz system, and a complete 8-archetype product world — built with zero budget and zero team.

Performance / Analytics

Performance & Metrics

Process

STEP 01

Concept & Naming

Developed the celestial archetype system. Named the brand "Rekha Ganita" — a Sanskrit term meaning "line geometry", referencing celestial paths and orbital lines.

STEP 02

Brand Identity

Designed the full visual identity — typography system, color palette per celestial, symbol set, and packaging concept. Each product has its own visual language within a unified system.

STEP 03

Product Design

Developed scent profiles for each celestial archetype. Designed packaging concept including label design, diffuser vessel, and reed presentation for each of the 8 variants.

STEP 04

Scent Oracle

Built a personality quiz that guides customers through questions about mood, environment, and sensory preference — then reveals their matched celestial archetype and product.

STEP 05

E-commerce Build

Built the full store on Wix E-commerce. Product pages, collection page, quiz flow, checkout, and brand story page — all designed and implemented solo.

STEP 06

Content & Launch

Wrote all brand copy — product descriptions, brand story, quiz questions, and email sequences. Launched with pre-order option while products are being produced.
